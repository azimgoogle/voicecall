## Notes for Ahmad

- **Don't mention the app name or show a screenshot** in the Facebook post — you want unbiased answers about the problem, not reactions to the solution.
- **Family Link angle**: Questions 10–12 specifically test whether parents see this as a standalone app or a companion to Family Link. If most say "complement", positioning it as a Family Link companion (rather than a competitor) is a strong go-to-market angle — Google won't build audio check-in into Family Link anytime soon.
- **Watch Q7 and Q8 closely** — these are your highest-signal questions. If 60%+ say "yes this has happened" on Q7 and "very useful" on Q8, you have validated pain.
- **The email field at the end** doubles as a waitlist. Anyone who fills it in is a warm lead for your first 50 beta testers.
